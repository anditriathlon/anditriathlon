[BLACKROLL® GROOVE STANDARD](https://shop.blackroll.com/collections/blackroll/products/blackroll-groove-standard) (orange)
===================

be carefull with this tool, because it may cause damage if used not in proper way! I use it only for glutes, hamstring and calfs and only for muscle stimulating massage not for recovery!


[BLACKROLL® MINI](https://shop.blackroll.com/collections/blackroll/products/blackroll-mini) (red small not on the picture)
===================

Using it mostly for:

* CALF AND ACHILLES TENDON - it works like a charm. I am using it always when I start feeling this characteristic pain in heel. The MED is too big for this. [HOW TO](https://www.blackroll.com/exercises/regeneration/blackroll-mini?show=122)

----------------

I strongly recommend [Janek Wacholski](https://www.facebook.com/jan.wacholski) - great physiotherapist, osteopath with very holistic approach. An ex physiotherapist of polish double Olympic Champion and a double World Champion in cross country skiing Justyna Kowalczyk
